What AI tools does Upscaliq train teams on?
15+ tools customised by department: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Midjourney, Canva AI, Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot 365, Perplexity, Make.com, DeepSeek, NotebookLM, ElevenLabs, and more. We never give a generic demo — every tool is chosen for that team's actual daily workflow.
How much time can AI training actually save?
Based on our engagements: QA teams save 50% of their time, design teams save 70%, and presales teams save 30% — typically within the first 10 days of adoption. These are measured results, not estimates.
Is AI training available online?
Yes. Upscaliq offers both in-person and fully online AI training globally. Sessions are equally hands-on and interactive in both formats — every participant practises in real-time.
We already use AI tools. Can Upscaliq still help?
Yes — this is where we often deliver the most value. Most teams use AI tools incorrectly or inefficiently. We audit their current usage, identify gaps, and train on the right tools for their workflows. One client reduced their AI subscriptions by 50% while improving productivity across every team.
How long is the training?
Training is customised. The full 10-day engagement covers all departments. Individual department training sessions range from half-day workshops (4 hours) to 3-day intensive programmes. We design the format based on your team's needs.
