What does Upscaliq do?
Upscaliq is an AI-first marketing and technology consultancy based in Ahmedabad, India. We provide AI training for corporate teams, digital marketing services, and IT consulting — all under one roof, without the cost of three separate agencies.
Does Upscaliq provide AI training in Ahmedabad?
Yes. Upscaliq provides hands-on AI tools training for corporate teams in Ahmedabad and across India. Training covers ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and AI workflows customised for each department — from sales and marketing to operations and HR. Sessions available in-person and online.

Upscaliq's AI training covers 15+ tools including ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Midjourney, Canva AI, Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot 365, Perplexity, Make.com, ElevenLabs, HeyGen, and more. Training is customised by department and business need.

Upscaliq offers SEO, social media marketing, content strategy, email marketing, and paid campaign management — all AI-augmented for faster, measurable results. We serve businesses across Ahmedabad and India.

Upscaliq was founded by Siddharth Vyas, who brings over 11 years of experience in enterprise SaaS, consulting, and AI-powered marketing. He personally leads all client engagements — you work directly with the founder, not a junior account manager.

Yes. Upscaliq is headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. We provide in-person services in Ahmedabad and across Gujarat, and online services for businesses across India — Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and beyond.
