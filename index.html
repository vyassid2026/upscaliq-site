Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Want
to Know About Upscaliq

Straight answers. Email support@upscaliq.com if yours is not here.

What exactly does Upscaliq do?
Upscaliq is an AI-first growth partner. We work in three areas — AI adoption training, digital marketing, and IT consulting — all under one team. Most clients engage us for the 10-day transformation workshop covering all three simultaneously.
How can you deliver results in just 10 days?
Because we don't start from zero. Our model: Day 1-2 is audit and roadmap. Days 3-8 are department-by-department implementation — AI training, process redesign, and marketing material delivery happening simultaneously. Days 9-10 are handover and documentation. Every day has specific deliverables. Nothing is theoretical.
What AI tools does Upscaliq train teams on?
15+ tools customised by department: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Midjourney, Canva AI, Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot 365, Perplexity, Make.com, DeepSeek, NotebookLM, ElevenLabs, HeyGen, and more. We train on tools relevant to each team's actual daily work — not generic demos.
We already use several AI tools. Can Upscaliq still help?
Yes — this is where we often deliver the most value. In a recent engagement, a company used 12+ AI tools with no clear ROI. After our audit they reduced to 6 tools, saved 50% on AI subscriptions, and their teams were genuinely productive. Having tools is not the same as using them well.
Does Upscaliq work with businesses outside India?
Yes. Upscaliq serves businesses worldwide — in-person where geography allows, and fully online globally. Our workshop model works equally well over video calls. We serve clients across India, the US, the Middle East, and beyond.
Who leads the engagement — is it junior staff?
Every engagement is led personally by Siddharth Vyas — founder of Upscaliq with 11+ years of enterprise SaaS and consulting experience. The person you speak to in the discovery call is the person who does the work.
What does Upscaliq deliver at the end of an engagement?
Typically: trained teams across all departments, documented AI workflows per role, brand guidelines and marketing materials, SEO-ready website recommendations, competitor analysis, sales scripts, demo decks, product enhancement roadmap, and a 30-day support window.

Fill out the contact form below or email support@upscaliq.com. We respond within 24 hours and schedule a free 30-minute discovery call. You leave the call with clarity on what the engagement looks like, what it costs, and what results to expect. No pitch. No pressure.
