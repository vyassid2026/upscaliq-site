About Upscaliq

Straight answers. If your question isn't here — email us directly.

What exactly does Upscaliq do?
Upscaliq is an AI-first growth partner. We work with businesses in three areas — AI adoption training for teams, digital marketing, and IT consulting — all under one team. Most clients engage us for the 10-day transformation workshop that covers all three simultaneously.
How can you deliver results in just 10 days?
Because we don't start from zero. We have a proven engagement model: Day 1-2 is audit and roadmap. Days 3-8 are department-by-department hands-on implementation — AI training, process redesign, and marketing material delivery happening simultaneously. Days 9-10 are handover and documentation. Every day has deliverables. Nothing is theoretical.
What AI tools does Upscaliq train teams on?
We train on 15+ tools customised by department. ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Midjourney, Canva AI, Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot 365, Perplexity, Make.com, DeepSeek, NotebookLM, ElevenLabs, HeyGen, and more. Critically — we do not recommend tools. We train your team on the specific tools relevant to their actual daily work.
Does Upscaliq work with businesses outside India?
Yes. Upscaliq serves businesses worldwide — in-person where geography allows, and fully online globally. Our structured workshop model works equally well over video calls. We have worked with clients across India, the US, and the Middle East.
We already use several AI tools. Can Upscaliq still help?
Yes — and this is where we often deliver the most value. In our last engagement, a company was using 12+ AI tools across departments with no clear ROI. After our audit, they reduced that to 6 tools, saved 50% on AI subscriptions, and their teams were actually productive with the tools they kept. Having tools is not the same as using them well.
What does Upscaliq deliver at the end of an engagement?
Depends on the scope, but typically: trained teams across all departments, documented AI workflows per role, brand guidelines and marketing materials, SEO-ready website recommendations, competitor analysis, sales scripts and demo decks, product enhancement roadmap, and a 30-day follow-up support window.
Who leads the engagement — is it junior staff?
Every engagement is led personally by Siddharth Vyas — founder of Upscaliq with 11+ years of enterprise SaaS and consulting experience. You will never be handed off to a junior team. The person you speak to in the discovery call is the person who does the work.
